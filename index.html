Kempton Park Technical Academy

Kempton Park Technical Academy provides high-quality, career-focused technical and tertiary education designed to close the gap between classroom theory and workplace practice. We deliver nationally accredited programmes across engineering, commerce and the humanities, emphasising hands-on learning, ethical leadership and employer-aligned skills so our graduates are ready to add immediate value.

We also support students studying UNISA modules and university-level courses by offering tutorial assistance, module-specific coaching, assignment guidance and exam preparation. Whether you are completing a distance-learning module, upgrading your qualifications or pursuing a new professional path, our teaching team provides structured support to help you succeed.

Our Tertiary Programmes

We provide tailored support for university modules — including UNISA distance-learning modules — and for university-level engineering courses. Our team offers focused tutorials and assignment support across core engineering streams (Civil and Electrical), helping students strengthen fundamentals, improve problem-solving and prepare confidently for assessments.

Whether you are working through an individual UNISA module or completing a multi-year university programme, our approach combines subject-matter expertise with practical examples and exam-focused guidance so you can progress efficiently and successfully.

Engineering, Science & Technology

  • N1 – N6 Engineering Studies
  • Engineering Mathematics, Science & Physics
  • Fluid Mechanics
  • Strength of Materials
  • Building & Structural Construction
  • Quantity Surveying
  • IT Systems & Network Administration
  • Programming & Software Development

These engineering, science and technology modules provide the technical foundations and applied skills required for engineering and technology careers. Students study core mathematics and physics, explore mechanics and material behaviour, and gain practical workshop experience in structural construction, fluid mechanics and systems engineering. The curriculum balances theory with laboratory and project work to develop problem-solving, technical drawing and hands-on competence.

Commerce & Financial Sciences

  • Accounting Science
  • Taxation
  • Financial Management
  • Auditing (Internal & External)
  • Quantitative Methods
  • Business Statistics

The commerce and financial sciences modules develop practical financial literacy and analytical skills. Topics include accounting principles, tax law and compliance, management of organisational finances, auditing methodologies and quantitative techniques. Students learn to interpret financial statements, assess fiscal performance and apply statistical tools to support business decision-making and risk management.

Humanities & Social Sciences

  • Law (Property, Land & Conveyancing)
  • Epistemology
  • International Relations
  • Philosophy

Humanities and social science modules cultivate critical thinking, ethical reasoning and an understanding of legal and societal systems. Students study property and conveyancing law, philosophical frameworks and international relations, building the capacity to analyse policy, reason clearly and communicate arguments effectively—skills that apply across public, legal and policy-focused careers.

About Us

Kempton Park Technical Academy was founded to bridge academic excellence with practical, career-ready skills. We deliver nationally accredited programmes across engineering, commerce and the humanities, designed in partnership with industry to ensure graduates can step confidently into the workplace. Our curriculum emphasises applied learning, ethical leadership and innovation so students leave prepared for real challenges and opportunities.

Our lecturers bring more than a decade of combined academic and industry experience to the classroom, blending rigorous scholarship with hands-on mentorship. With small class sizes, personalised support and real-world projects, our teaching team focuses on transforming potential into professional competence — empowering students to lead with integrity, creativity and technical excellence.

We provide a comprehensive student support system that includes one-on-one tutorial sessions, module clinics and flexible timetabling for part-time and working students. Our facilities support blended learning — students can attend practical workshops on campus, access curated online resources, and receive targeted remedial help if they need to bring specific skills up to standard.

The Academy maintains close links with regional employers and higher education partners to keep our courses relevant and to create pathways for internships, work placements and further study. We also assist distance learners by providing structured guidance for UNISA modules and other university-level coursework, helping students to manage assignments, prepare for exams and navigate academic requirements with confidence.
